## 1 Thessalonians 1:3 (id: 165728)

**What does Paul always remember before God concerning the Thessalonians?**

Paul remembers their work of faith, their labor of love, and their patience of hope.

* **Associated Passages:** 1TH 1:3

## 1 Thessalonians 1:5 (id: 165729)

**In what four ways did the gospel come to the Thessalonians?**

The gospel came to the Thessalonians in word, in power, in the Holy Spirit, and in much assurance.

* **Associated Passages:** 1TH 1:5

## 1 Thessalonians 1:6 (id: 165730)

**What was happening to the Thessalonians as they received the word of the gospel?**

The Thessalonians received the word in much hardship.

* **Associated Passages:** 1TH 1:6

## 1 Thessalonians 1:6 (#2) (id: 165731)

**What was the Thessalonians’ attitude as they received the word of the gospel?**

* **Associated Passages:** 1TH 1:6

## 1 Thessalonians 1:8 (id: 165732)
