## Esther 1:1 (id: 168684)

**Over how much territory did Ahasuerus reign?**

Ahasuerus ruled from India even as far as Ethiopia, 127 provinces.

* **Associated Passages:** EST 1:1

## Esther 1:3 (id: 168685)

**Who was in the king’s presence?**

The officials of the provinces were “before his face,” meaning that they were in the king’s presence.

* **Associated Passages:** EST 1:3

**What did Memukan say the women of Persia and Media would do?**
